App Description:
A voting application that leverages the blockchain technology for security. The app allows users to cast their votes and prevents duplicate votes with the help of hashing. Every vote is equivalent to a block in a blockchain and has it's own unique hash. If a vote is tampered with, its hash is altered and the blockchain breaks.
